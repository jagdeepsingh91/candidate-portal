NOTICE RELATING TO THE PERSONAL DATA PROTECTION ACT 2014

In view of the Personal Data Protection Act taking effect from 2nd July 2014, we would like to keep you informed that Raffles Institution is committed to ensuring that your personal information submitted to us will be kept strictly confidential. We will seek your consent if we will be using your personal information for any purpose other than recruitment. If you have any queries or complaints relating to this Notice or otherwise relating to misuse or suspected misuse of your personal information, you may send us an email to rihr@ri.edu.sg
